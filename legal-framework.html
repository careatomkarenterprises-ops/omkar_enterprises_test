Institutional Legal Protocols

Your capital is protected by a multi-layered legal framework designed for maximum security.

The Indian Contract Act

Every transaction is documented under the Indian Contract Act, 1872, ensuring the enforceability of your principal repayment and monthly payouts.

Notarized Documentation

All loan agreements are executed on stamp paper and notarized by authorized officials, providing a permanent legal record of your investment.

Post-Dated Cheques (PDC)

As a primary security layer, Omkar Enterprises issues Post-Dated Cheques for the total principal amount, providing an extra tier of financial safety.
