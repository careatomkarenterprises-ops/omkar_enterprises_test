Tier FAQs

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Additional investment follows the same security protocol with fresh PDCs and supplementary agreement.

Is there a minimum lock-in period?

All tiers have an 8-month minimum partnership period. Early withdrawal before 8 months results in recalculated returns at 1.5% monthly rate.

How are returns calculated for partial months?

Returns are calculated on a pro-rata basis for partial months. Your first returns are paid on the 5th of the following month.
