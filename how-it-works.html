Our Onboarding Protocol

A transparent 4-step journey to securing your monthly payouts.

1

Consultation & KYC

Initial meeting to understand your goals. We collect basic KYC documents (Aadhar, PAN) to verify eligibility.

2

Legal Execution

Preparation of the Loan Agreement on appropriate stamp paper. Documentation is notarized for legal validity.

3

Security Issuance

Omkar Enterprises issues Post-Dated Cheques (PDCs) and the original notarized copy to the partner.

4

Monthly Payouts

Capital deployment begins. You receive 2.5% payouts directly to your bank account every 30 days.
