How It Works

Transparent, simple process from inquiry to monthly returns

01

Initial Inquiry & Consultation

Begin with a conversation about your investment goals and our structured private lending agreements.

  • Confidential discussion of investment objectives
  • Review of structured agreement terms
  • Clear explanation of 2.5% fixed monthly returns
  • Q&A session addressing all queries
Timeframe: 1-3 days
02

Agreement Finalization & Capital Deployment

Documentation and formal agreement with transparent terms and secure fund transfer.

  • Receive draft agreement for review
  • Finalize terms and sign agreement
  • Secure capital transfer via banking channels
  • Receive confirmation and agreement copies
Timeframe: 3-5 days
03

Monthly Returns & Portfolio Management

Consistent returns and transparent reporting through our investor portal.

  • Receive 2.5% returns monthly by 10th working day
  • Access 24/7 investor portal for tracking
  • Regular portfolio updates and statements
  • Annual principal repayment (13-month term)
Ongoing: 13-month cycle
