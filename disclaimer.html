Risk Disclaimer

Important Information About Investment Risks

IMPORTANT WARNING

This document contains important risk information. Please read it carefully before considering any investment with Omkar Enterprises.

1. NOT A REGULATED SCHEME

Omkar Enterprises' private lending partnerships are:

  • NOT a bank deposit or savings account
  • NOT insured by DICGC or any insurance scheme
  • NOT regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
  • NOT regulated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
  • NOT a collective investment scheme
  • NOT a guaranteed return scheme
  • NOT covered by the Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019 as we enter into private loan agreements

2. NATURE OF TRANSACTION

Our services involve:

  • Private loan agreements between Omkar Enterprises and individual lenders
  • Bilateral contracts governed by Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Structured lending with fixed monthly returns
  • Post-dated cheques as security mechanism
  • 13-month investment terms with bullet principal repayment

3. KEY RISK FACTORS

3.1 Capital Risk

Your entire investment capital is at risk. While secured by post-dated cheques and contractual obligations, there is no guarantee of full repayment. You could lose some or all of your investment.

3.2 Liquidity Risk

Investments are locked in for a minimum of 8 months. Early withdrawal is subject to penalties and may involve delays. There is no secondary market for these investments.

3.3 Credit Risk

The ability to repay depends on Omkar Enterprises' business performance and financial health. While we maintain robust business practices, economic conditions can affect repayment capacity.

3.4 Market Risk

Returns depend on our investment performance in various business activities. Market fluctuations, economic changes, and business risks can affect returns.

3.5 Enforcement Risk

While post-dated cheques provide legal recourse under the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, enforcement may involve:

  • Time delays in legal proceedings
  • Legal costs and expenses
  • Uncertainty in recovery amount
  • Procedural complexities

3.6 Concentration Risk

Your investment is concentrated with a single entity (Omkar Enterprises). Diversification is an important risk management strategy that may not be achieved through this investment alone.

4. RETURN CONSIDERATIONS

Important considerations regarding returns:

  • 2.5% monthly returns are contractually obligated, not guaranteed
  • Returns depend on business performance
  • Past performance does not indicate future results
  • Higher returns typically involve higher risks
  • Inflation may erode real returns
  • Tax implications affect net returns

5. LEGAL CONSIDERATIONS

Important legal aspects to understand:

  • Loan agreements are binding legal contracts
  • Disputes resolved through arbitration in Pune
  • Limited liability: Claims against company assets only
  • 90-day grace period for payments
  • Early exit conditions and penalties apply
  • Governing law: Indian laws and courts

6. SUITABILITY ASSESSMENT

This investment may be suitable for you if you:

  • Have surplus funds not required for daily expenses
  • Understand and accept the risks involved
  • Have a diversified investment portfolio
  • Have consulted with financial and legal advisors
  • Have adequate emergency funds
  • Are investing for the long term
  • Can afford to lose the invested capital

7. INVESTOR DECLARATION

By proceeding with an investment, you confirm that:

  • You have read and understood this Risk Disclaimer
  • You have consulted with independent financial and legal advisors
  • You understand the risks involved
  • You are investing based on your own judgment
  • You can afford to lose the invested amount
  • You are not relying solely on our representations
  • You have adequate knowledge and experience in financial matters

8. DOCUMENTATION REVIEW

We strongly recommend:

  • Reviewing the complete Loan Agreement with your legal advisor
  • Understanding all clauses and implications
  • Asking questions about anything unclear
  • Keeping copies of all documents
  • Regularly reviewing your investment
