Investment Plan FAQs

Common questions about our investment tiers

What happens after 13 months?

At the end of the 13-month term, your principal is returned, and you can choose to renew your investment, increase your amount, or withdraw your funds.

Can I invest additional amounts?

Yes, you can make additional investments at any time. Each new investment starts a fresh 13-month cycle.

What is the performance bonus?

Premium tier investors may receive an additional 0.5% return based on overall portfolio performance and tenure.

How are returns taxed?

Returns are subject to applicable income tax laws in India. We provide necessary documentation for tax filing purposes.

What is the lock-in period?

The initial 8 months are a lock-in period where early withdrawal may be subject to terms specified in your agreement.

Can I upgrade my tier?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher tier when your investment reaches the minimum threshold for that tier.
