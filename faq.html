General Questions

Omkar Enterprises facilitates private lending agreements between investors and carefully selected business initiatives. We provide structured investment opportunities with fixed monthly returns of 2.5% over a 13-month term.

No, this is NOT a bank deposit, NOT a mutual fund, and NOT regulated by RBI/SEBI as a deposit-taking entity. We facilitate private loan agreements between individuals and businesses. Returns depend on business performance and are not guaranteed by any government agency.

Individuals aged 18 years or older with a valid PAN card and Indian bank account can invest. We work with both new and experienced investors looking for structured private lending opportunities.

Investment Process

The process is simple: 1) Schedule a consultation to discuss your goals, 2) Review and sign the private lending agreement, 3) Transfer funds as per agreement, 4) Start receiving monthly returns.

You'll need: PAN card copy, Aadhaar card copy, cancelled cheque/bank statement, passport-size photograph, and KYC documents as per regulatory requirements.

Typically 3-5 working days after document submission. This includes agreement finalization, verification, and fund transfer setup.

Currently, we only accept individual investments. However, family members can invest separately and manage their portfolios individually.

Returns & Payments

Returns are calculated at 2.5% per month on the invested capital. For example: ₹1,00,000 investment yields ₹2,500 monthly (₹1,00,000 × 2.5%).

Monthly returns are credited by the 10th working day of each month to your registered bank account.

Each investment has a 13-month term: 12 months of returns and principal repayment in the 13th month.

There's an 8-month lock-in period. Early withdrawal before this period may be subject to terms specified in your agreement.

Yes, returns are subject to income tax as per Indian tax laws. We provide Form 16A for TDS deducted (if applicable) and annual returns statements for tax filing.

Security & Legal

While we implement robust security measures and due diligence, it's important to understand that private lending carries inherent risks. Returns depend on business performance. We recommend consulting with financial and legal advisors before investing.

Agreements are structured under the Indian Contract Act, 1872. Each agreement clearly outlines terms, conditions, and dispute resolution mechanisms.
