Transparency & FAQs

Clear answers to your most important security questions.

Is this legally compliant?

Yes. We operate under the Indian Contract Act, 1872. Every partnership is governed by a notarized Loan Agreement that clearly defines the principal, interest, and tenure.

How is my capital secured?

Capital is secured through Post-Dated Cheques (PDCs) issued at the time of funding, acting as a secondary layer of security alongside the legal contract.

When are payouts credited?

Returns are credited every 30 days from the date of capital deployment directly into your registered bank account.
