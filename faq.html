Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our private lending partnerships

General Questions

What is Omkar Enterprises?

Omkar Enterprises is a proprietary investment firm that enters into structured private lending partnerships with individuals. We provide fixed monthly returns through comprehensive loan agreements with legal security mechanisms.

Is this a regulated investment scheme?

No. Omkar Enterprises engages in private loan agreements with individuals. This is NOT a bank deposit, NOT insured, NOT regulated by RBI/SEBI as a deposit-taking entity, and NOT a guaranteed return scheme. We operate as a proprietary investment firm under private lending arrangements.

Who can invest with Omkar Enterprises?

To invest, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Be an Indian resident
  • Have valid KYC documents (PAN, Aadhaar)
  • Have a bank account in your name
  • Understand and accept the risks involved
  • Invest based on your own financial assessment
What is the minimum and maximum investment?

Minimum investment: ₹25,000
Maximum investment: ₹25,00,000
We offer four investment tiers: Emerging (₹25K-₹99K), Growth (₹1L-₹5L), Premium (₹5L-₹10L), and Elite (₹10L-₹25L).

How long has Omkar Enterprises been operating?

Omkar Enterprises was established in 2018 and has been successfully managing private lending partnerships since then. We have maintained a consistent track record of on-time payments and transparent operations.

Investment Process

What is the investment process?

The 3-step process:

  1. Consultation: Initial discussion and tier selection
  2. Agreement: Review and sign comprehensive loan agreement
  3. Funding: Transfer funds and receive PDCs as security

Monthly returns begin the following month, with principal returned in the 13th month.

What documents are required?

Required documents include:

  • PAN Card copy
  • Aadhaar Card copy (masked)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Bank account details (cancelled cheque or statement)
  • 6-month bank statements for source verification
How long does the process take?

Typically 5-7 working days:

  • Consultation scheduling: 1-2 days
  • Documentation review: 1-2 days
  • Agreement execution: 1 day
  • Fund transfer and PDC issuance: 1-2 days

The investment period itself is 13 months from the date of agreement.

Can I invest jointly with someone?

Currently, we only accept individual investments. Each partnership is between Omkar Enterprises and an individual lender. However, you can consult with us about structuring investments for family situations.

What happens after I submit the initial inquiry?

After submitting an inquiry:

  1. We contact you within 24 hours to schedule a consultation
  2. During consultation, we explain the process and answer questions
  3. If interested, we provide sample documents for review
  4. You review documents with your advisors
  5. Upon decision, we proceed with formal agreement

Returns & Payments

What returns can I expect?

We offer:

  • Fixed Returns: 2.5% monthly (30% annual) contractually obligated
  • Performance Returns: Variable additional returns based on business performance
  • Total Return: Principal returned in the 13th month via bullet payment

Example: ₹10,00,000 investment yields ₹25,000 monthly fixed returns.

How and when are payments made?

Payment schedule:

  • Monthly Returns: Paid on the 1st of each month via NEFT/IMPS
  • Performance Returns: Paid quarterly or annually as applicable
  • Principal Return: Paid in the 13th month via PDC
  • Payment Proof: Payment confirmations sent via email/SMS

All payments are made from our official bank account.

Are returns guaranteed?

The 2.5% monthly return is contractually obligated under our Loan Agreement, not "guaranteed" in the regulatory sense. We are legally bound to pay it as per the agreed terms. However, like all investments, there is risk involved.

What is the tax treatment of returns?

Returns are treated as interest income:

  • Taxable under "Income from Other Sources"
  • TDS may be deducted as per applicable laws
  • We provide Form 16A for tax filing
  • Investors should consult their tax advisors
  • Principal repayment is not taxable

Tax laws are subject to change. Consult your tax advisor for current implications.

What happens if a payment is delayed?

Our agreement includes:

  • 90-day grace period for payments
  • Clear communication about any delays
  • Penalty provisions for significant delays
  • PDC enforcement option after 90+ days
  • Arbitration process for disputes

We have maintained 100% on-time payment record to date.

Security & Legal

What security do I have for my investment?

Multiple layers of security:

  • Post-Dated Cheques: For principal amount, enforceable under NI Act
  • Comprehensive Agreement: 10-page legally binding loan agreement
  • Arbitration Clause: Mandatory arbitration in Pune for disputes
  • Business Reputation: Established track record and transparency
  • Legal Framework: Clear terms and enforcement mechanisms
Can I exit before 13 months?

Yes, with conditions:

  • Minimum 8-month lock-in period
  • Returns recalculated at 1.5% monthly for actual period
  • Excess payments treated as partial principal repayment
  • 90-day processing period for early exit
  • Mutual agreement required
  • No exit allowed in first 8 months
What happens in case of default?

Default process:

  1. 90-day grace period from due date
  2. Written notice and opportunity to cure
  3. If unresolved, PDCs become enforceable
  4. Legal recourse under Negotiable Instruments Act
  5. Arbitration initiation if needed
  6. Claims limited to company assets only
Is there a cooling-off period?

We offer a 7-day review period:

  • 7 days from agreement receipt to review with advisors
  • No funds transferred during this period
  • Opportunity to ask questions and clarify terms
  • Agreement becomes binding only after fund transfer
  • No obligation until funds are transferred
How are disputes resolved?

Dispute resolution process:

  1. Written notice of dispute
  2. 30-day amicable resolution period
  3. If unresolved, mandatory arbitration in Pune
  4. Arbitration under Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996
  5. Arbitrator's decision is final and binding
  6. Costs as per arbitrator's decision

Password recovery process:

Contact information updates:

How are my documents stored and protected?

Document protection measures:

  • Secure physical storage for originals
  • Access controls and audit trails
  • Regular backups and security updates
  • Compliance with data protection norms
  • 7-year retention period as per regulations

  • 99.9% uptime maintained with monitoring
